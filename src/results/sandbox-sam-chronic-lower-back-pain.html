What they presented with

Daily lower back pain rated 8/10. Neck tension 7/10. Sleep disrupted, running stopped 2 years ago. Standard imaging showed mild L5/S1 disc bulge but nothing requiring intervention.

What we focused on

Gait retraining, breath mechanics, hip rotation drills, and progressive lateral line conditioning over 36 sessions.

How they feel now

Lower back pain dropped from 8/10 to 1/10. Neck tension from 7/10 to 2/10. Back to running 5km three times a week. Sleeping through the night without waking from pain.
“I'd given up on running. Twelve months later, my back doesn't hurt and I'm doing things I thought I'd lost for good. The work is honest — it's not a magic fix, it's a method.”

Clinical Takeaways

Practitioner notes

Chronic lower back presentations with no surgical indication respond well to gait-driven retraining when sustained over 6+ months. The lateral line work was the unlock here — once Sam could rotate through the hip without compensating through the lumbar spine, the pain pattern dissolved.
